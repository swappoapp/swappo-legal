## 2. Data We Collect
Based on the functionality of the Swappo app, we collect the following personal data:

* **Identity Data:** First name, last name, username (`UserModel`).
* **Contact Data:** Email address, phone number, and optional physical shipping addresses (`AddressModel`).
* **Profile Data:** Profile picture, biography/description, language preferences, and your "Favorites" list.
* **Transaction & Economy Data:** Token balance (`tokenBalance`), transaction history (`TransactionModel`), and orders.
* **Content Data:** Photos and descriptions of items you upload for exchange (`ProductModel`), reviews, and ratings you give or receive.
* **Technical Data:** Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens for push notifications (`fcmToken`), device type, and login timestamps (`createdAt`, `updatedAt`).
* **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the app, including users you have blocked (`blockedUsers`).

## 3. How We Use Your Data
We process your data for the following purposes:

* **Service Provision:** To create your account, manage your inventory, and facilitate swaps/exchanges between users.
* **Communication:** To send order updates, chat messages between users, and push notifications regarding your account activity.
* **Security:** To verify accounts (via email/phone), manage blocked users, and prevent fraud.
* **Improvement:** To analyze usage trends (e.g., popular categories) to improve the app experience.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)
* **Contractual Necessity:** Processing your name, email, and transaction data is necessary to fulfill the service (swapping items).
* **Consent:** You explicitly consent to optional data (e.g., uploading a profile picture or adding a shipping address).
* **Legitimate Interest:** We process data to maintain app security and prevent abuse.

## 5. Data Sharing & Third Parties
We do not sell your data. However, we use trusted third-party service providers to operate the app:

* **Google Firebase:** Used for authentication, database hosting (Firestore), cloud storage (images), and notifications.
* **Google Analytics:** (If applicable) Used to analyze app performance.

## 6. User Rights
Under GDPR, you have the right to:

* **Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
* **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data (editable via your Profile settings).
* **Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** Request deletion of your account and data. You can do this within the app settings.
* **Restriction:** Restrict how we process your data.

## 7. Data Retention
We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile data is removed from our live database, though some transaction records may be kept for legal/tax reporting purposes if required by law.
