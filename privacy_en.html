SWAPPO PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 20/12/2025

1. Data Controller

The entity responsible for your data is:

  • Name: Lorenzo Concina & Michelangelo Concina
  • Contact Email: support@swappoapp.it
  • Location: Trento, Italy

2. Data We Collect

Based on the features of the Swappo app, we collect the following personal data:

  • Identity Data: Name, surname, username (UserModel).
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, and optional physical shipping addresses (AddressModel).
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, biography/description, language preferences, and your "Favorites" list.
  • Transaction and Economic Data: Token balance (tokenBalance), transaction history (TransactionModel), and orders.
  • Content Data: Photos and descriptions of items uploaded for exchange (ProductModel), reviews, and ratings provided or received.
  • Technical Data: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications (fcmToken), device type, and access timestamps (createdAt, updatedAt).
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use the app, including users you have blocked (blockedUsers).

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To create your account, manage your inventory, and facilitate exchanges between users.
  • Communication: To send order updates, chat messages between users, and push notifications regarding your account activity.
  • Security: To verify accounts (via email/phone), manage blocked users, and prevent fraud.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage trends (e.g., popular categories) in order to improve the app experience.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing your name, email, and transaction data is necessary to fulfill the service (exchanging items).
  • Consent: You explicitly consent to the processing of optional data (e.g., uploading a profile picture or adding a shipping address).
  • Legitimate Interest: We process data to maintain app security and prevent abuse.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your data. However, we use trusted third-party service providers to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, database hosting (Firestore), cloud storage (images), and notifications.
  • Google Analytics: (If applicable) Used to analyze app performance.

6. User Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data (modifiable via your Profile settings).
  • Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request the deletion of your account and data. You can do this within the app settings.
  • Restriction: Restrict how we process your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile data is removed from our active database, although some transaction records may be retained for legal/tax purposes if required by law.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at: support@swappoapp.it
